*   **Audio**: Minimalist, high-tech electronic beat.

## 💡 Key Strategic Shifts
1.  **Manual-First**: Instead of just a "Download" link, pitch it as: *"Read the Blueprint of Future Hospitality."* It makes the document sound like a valuable resource.
2.  **The "Sandbox" Angle**: Tell people they can use the **demo logins** (`admin@grand.com`, etc.) to play around without breaking anything. It removes the friction of "registering."
3.  **Community Building**: Reply to every comment. This is UAT—people need to know the developer is listening.
